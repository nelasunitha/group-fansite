Aromatic Biryani Delights

Vegetarian Biryani

Mixed Vegetable Biryani

A vibrant and aromatic dish with basmati rice cooked to perfection, layered with fresh vegetables like carrots, beans, peas, and cauliflower. Flavored with traditional Indian spices, saffron, and ghee, it is garnished with crispy fried onions, roasted cashews, and fresh mint leaves, offering a wholesome and colorful experience.

Paneer Biryani

Soft cubes of paneer(cottage cheese) marinated in yogurt and spices, layered with fragrant basmati rice, caramelized onions, and saffron strands. This biryani has a rich and creamy texture, infused with the flavors of cardamom, cinnamon, and bay leaves, making it a perfect vegetarian delight.

Mushroom Biryani

Tender, earthy mushrooms cooked in a spiced masala gravy and layered with fragrant basmati rice. The dish is enriched with subtle notes of garlic, ginger, and fresh coriander, topped with crispy onions and a squeeze of lemon for a burst of flavor.

Non-vegetarian Biryani

Chicken Biryani

A classic and flavorful dish, chicken biryani is made with tender chicken pieces marinated in a blend of yogurt, turmeric, and traditional spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The marinated chicken is cooked and layered with fragrant basmati rice, saffron-infused milk, caramelized onions, and fresh mint leaves.

Lamb Biryani

Succulent pieces of lamb slow-cooked in a spiced masala, layered with aromatic basmati rice, saffron, and a touch of ghee. The lamb is tender and flavorful, and the dish is topped with fried onions, fresh coriander, and roasted cashews for an indulgent feast.

Shrimp Biryani

A delightful seafood delicacy, shrimp biryani is prepared with succulent shrimp marinated in a blend of tangy yogurt, turmeric, and aromatic spices like fennel, cumin, and coriander. The shrimp are cooked to perfection and layered with fragrant basmati rice infused with saffron and ghee.
